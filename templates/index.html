Welcome to the Bike Share Trip Planner!

This app uses current station data, along with predictions generated by a Gradient Boosting Tree model, to predict bike and dock availability for stations in the CitiBike system. It will direct you to the ideal station to get a bike at start of your trip, as well as the ideal station to dock your bike at the end. So, enter your start and end locations, and enjoy your trip!
